About Me

Hi! My name is Charles Gale.

I am a CS major at Kent State University, Ohio. A little about me, I am the oldest of 7 children. Some of my favorite hobbies are running, reading, and playing video games. I ran cross-country competitively for 10 years from 3rd grade until I graduated highschool in 2023. After graduation, I started training for marathons and have since completed three.

At Kent State, I am also part of the AFROTC program. This is a program designed to train students to become officers in the United States Air Force. I have completed my Field Training requirement and have contracted meaning I have proved myself and will join the air force as an officer upon graduation. In this program I was fortunate enough to be accepted into a summer program called SWOC (Special Warfare Orientation Course). This is a two week course at the Air Force Academy in Colorado that is designed to educate cadets on the requirements and lifestyle of the 19ZX (Special Warfare) career field.

It is my hope to commission into the Air Force as a 17X (Cyber Officer). My current degree and possible intership, I will be applying this December, will set me up for success in this career.
